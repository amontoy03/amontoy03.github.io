Website Evaluations

Good Website:

Louis Vuitton

Louis Vuitton is a luxury fashion brand that offers high-end clothing and accessories for both men and women.

  • Contrast: The site stands out with bold text and high-quality images on a clean, simple background, making it visually appealing and easy to navigate.
  • Repetition: Louis Vuitton keeps a consistent look with its branding, fonts, and layout, making the experience feel smooth and cohesive experience.
  • Alignment: The website maintains strong alignment with well-structured grids, ensuring a visually balanced layout.
  • Proximity: Product images and descriptions are placed together in a logical way, making the site easy to browse and navigate.

Bad Website:

Craigslist

Craigslist is a website where users can buy, sell, and trade items, find jobs, and connect with local communities.

  • Contrast: The site lacks strong contrast, using basic black text on a white background with minimal visual structure.
  • Repetition: Although the layout is consistent, it feels repetitive and outdated, showing little visual progress over time.
  • Alignment: The text and elements are properly aligned, but the overall layout feels outdated, lacking a modern and engaging design.
  • Proximity: Information is crammed together in tight spaces, making it difficult to read and overwhelming for users.
